# CEO Agent Brief

Review MCP Doctor as CEO oversight.

Your job is to tell the agents what they got wrong and what to do better.

Judge:

1. Is the product narrow enough to ship fast?
2. Is it useful enough to charge for?
3. Did the agents choose the right user?
4. Are there features that look smart but do not matter yet?
5. What must change before implementation?

End with a short decision:

- HOLD SCOPE
- CUT SCOPE
- EXPAND SCOPE

Include the exact changes the team should make.
